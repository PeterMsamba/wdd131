Site Purpose

The **EcoConnect Hub** site serves as an informative resource and community platform. It provides actionable guides, local event listings, and product reviews focused on zero-waste living, renewable energy, and ethical consumerism. Its primary goal is to empower visitors to adopt more sustainable habits and connect them with local green initiatives.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  1. **Scenario 1:** "I am new to zero-waste living. Where can I find a simple, step-by-step guide to start reducing plastic in my home?"
  2. **Scenario 2:** "I want to buy a high-quality, ethically-sourced travel mug. Does the site have a product review section, and which brand is recommended?"
  3. **Scenario 3:** "Are there any local community garden workshops or clean-up events happening in my area next month?"

Color Schema

Color Name Hex/RGB Value Usage
**Primary Green** `#38761D` (RGB: 56, 118, 29) **Headings, Buttons (Background), Logo Accent, Footer Background.** Represents nature, growth, and sustainability.
**Secondary Light Green** `#D9EAD3` (RGB: 217, 234, 211) **Section Backgrounds, Navigation Background, Accents.** Used for visual separation of content blocks.
**Background/Body** `#FFFFFF` (White) **Main Page Background, Card Backgrounds.** Provides clean readability.
**Text/Link** `#333333` (Dark Gray) **Body Text, Footer Text.** Ensures high contrast and readability against the white background.
